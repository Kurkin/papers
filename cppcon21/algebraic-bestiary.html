An Algebraic Bestiary
Sums, Products, Exponents, Monoids, Functors, Oh My! Why Should I Care?—Or—Abstract Nonsense in Practice.

C++Con Proposal

Proposal submissions due July 19

Conference October 24-29 2021

Abstract

This talk will outline various algebraic types and structures, such as sum product and exponential types, structures such as monoids and groups, and higher order structures such as functors and monads, and in particular why an API designer should be aware of them and supply operations that respect the laws of those structures. Doing so provides least surprising APIs that can be more widely reused.
